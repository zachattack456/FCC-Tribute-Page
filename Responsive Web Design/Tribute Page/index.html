Mike Howe

Vocalist for Metal Church

Mike Howe on stage with Metal Church

Mike Howe is the vocalist for a band called Metal Church. Below is an outline of this career.

  • 1965: Mike is born in Taylor, Michigan.
  • Beginning: Sings for the band Hellion in Detroit, Michigan.
  • 1986: Joins Heretic.
  • Early 1988: Puts out Breaking Point with Heretic under the Metal Blade label.
  • Late 1988: Joins Metal Church, replacing David Wayne.
  • 1989: Metal Church releases Blessing in Disguise under Elektra Records.
  • 1991: Metal Church realeases The Human Factor under Epic Records.
  • 1993: Hanging in the Balance is released under Blackheart Records.
  • 1996: Mike leaves Metal Church and moves to Tennessee with his family.
  • 1997: Mike sings vocals for Megora on thier EP Illusions.
  • 1998: Kurdt Vanderhoof of Metal Church reports Mike has retired.
  • 2006: I meet Mike Howe, who is living in northern California as my grandmother's neighbor.
  • 2015: Mike rejoins Metal Church.
  • 2016: XI is released under Rat Pack Records.
  • 2017: Classic Live is released, again under Rat Pack Records.
  • 2018: Damned If You Do is released, once agian under Rat Pack Records.
If you want to know more, I would suggest checking his Wikipedia page.
